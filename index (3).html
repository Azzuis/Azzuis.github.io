About Us

Built by two people who kept forgetting things.

We're a small electronics startup on a simple mission: make everyday life a little smoother. Not with loud alarms or constant screen notifications — but with soft, ambient LED cues embedded in the objects you already use.

Think of it as giving your home a gentle memory. A warm glow on your keys when you haven't locked the door. A subtle pulse on your coffee mug when a meeting is starting. Friction, reduced.

We're early, we're scrappy, and we're building something we genuinely believe in.
